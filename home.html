Robert J. Wyndham and his team are always prepared to help you with the unexpected.

Nobody anticipates being sued, being injured, being out of work, or being charged with a crime. When these unwelcome events find you, you should find me. Robert J. Wyndham has over 23 years of legal experience and will work tirelessly to protect your rights and interests. Whether you are facing a situation that can be resolved through aggressive negotiation, or your circumstances require me to proceed to trial, I will zealously advocate for you every step of the way.

Not all cases end up in court. Indeed, the vast majority of my cases do not. I am convinced, however, that my willingness and ability to go to trial helps me get better results for my clients. My experience is that insurance companies are formidable opponents who are ruthless when it comes to protecting their bottom line. They engage in negotiations to save money, not to do the right thing. Knowing that I am ready, willing, and able to go to court if necessary, helps me avoid a trial in most instances. I negotiate on an equal footing because I know the law and are not intimidated by the threat of going to trial.
